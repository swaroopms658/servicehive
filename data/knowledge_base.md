# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview
AutoStream is a SaaS tool for content creators that automates video editing workflows.

## Pricing and Features
### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Includes 10 videos per month
- Export resolution: 720p

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Includes unlimited videos
- Export resolution: 4K
- Includes AI captions

## Company Policies
- No refunds after 7 days.
- 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan.
